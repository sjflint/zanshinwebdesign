About Zanshin Web Design

Zanshin web design is a new start-up business founded by Simon Flint. We are here to provide clean, stylish and professional looking websites to local businesses and organisations.

  • Zanshin Web Design was founded by Simon, with a clear aim of providing professional websites at affordable prices.
  • Zanshin Web Design is just starting out. We are a brand new business. Come and join us as we help you build your dream business.
  • Zanshin is a Japanese martial arts term meaning a calm and focused mind, particularly during the pressure of combat. Simon is a 4th dan black belt in karate and brings this same philosophy to the building of websites.
